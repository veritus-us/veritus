Welcome to Veritus

We specialize in fully digital notarizations for your ease and time savings. Allow us to help you get your documents notarized without even having to leave you home or office.

Notarizations can be done on any computer, cell phone, or tablet. Security is a top priority, so we offer the latest in secure technologies including digital certificates for tamper-evident sealing, knowledge based authentication, biometrics, and encrypted video sessions.

Services

RON

Remote Online
Notarization $25+

Get your documents notarized at any time, on any device, over the internet. Simply connect through the emailed link at the scheduled time, upload your documents, complete your notarization. The quickest and most cost-effective option.

IPEN

In Person Electronic
Notarization $35+

We meet you in person and have your signature applied and notarized physically on an electronic device. Best for those not ready to make the jump to a fully digital experience.

Traditional

Ink and Paper
Notarization $35+

This is a traditional, standard notarization. We meet you in person an have your signature and our notary stamp applied in ink. While we strive for efficiency and paperless transactions, we do offer this services for those that few cases that require it.
